Frequently Asked Questions
What type of service do you perform?
We offer end to end mobility solutions to corporates for their female employees. We have sedan cars and all trained and licensed female/male drivers.
Can we place an ad hoc request for weekend travel of our female employees?
Absolutely, Yes request can be placed 48 hrs. in advance for weekend travel of employees.
Do you also provide female drivers for night shift?
No, female drivers will be available for day shift only. We have male drivers working in night-shift.
Do you also offer intercity airport to airport transfer for female employees?
Yes, we are planning to start intercity airport to airport transfer for female employees soon.
How easy is it to start your cab service?
We offer end to end solution (rostering, routing, compliance, tracking, MIS, Billing, etc.) to our client. You just need to share the employee details and their timings with us to get started.
How safe is your cab service?
We have GPS live tracking & Panic Button enabled for all our cars. Moreover, all our chauffeurs are well trained on how to use SOS features on their phones for sending emergency alerts.
Are there any additional cost apart from daily or monthly package?
Yes, we charge nominal fee, if you want to avail our end-to-end solution benefits.
In which area you operate?
Our operation is Pan Pune.
