#Extensive Driver Training
We have tied up with GDS for training our Fresher drivers, GDS is an A-graded RTO-approved school with the best infrastructure. Their training includes theory classes, simulator sessions, road practical sessions, and ghat sessions. We have started a batch for Fresher drivers so that they can train, learn and earn with us. We have identified passionate women who want to opt for driving as their profession and want to learn to drive. These women are enrolled in our training batch and are trained by our school, once they pass the regressive rounds of tests, they are onboarded with us as permanent ChauffHERS.

Our new EV charging station is now open at BNY Mellon campus in partnership with Jio-bp. This marks a milestone in our sustainability journey and commitment to reducing our carbon footprint.
